Introducing the ultimate sales booster for your WooCommerce store ‚Äì a smart popup product recommendations gallery of variation swatches that automatically converts your online store into a virtual physical store. With the help of popular variation swatches, it transforms your online store to look just like a physical store. This advanced add-on works seamlessly with all the popular WooCommerce variation swatches plugins to give your customers a shopping experience that's as close to real-life as possible.

=== How Does It Work? ===
Imagine if your store could provide the same instant experience as a physical store, where the shopkeeper could show you exactly what you're looking for in an instant.

Now, with <strong>Variation Swatches Smart Products Gallery for WooCommerce</strong>, you can create that same seamless experience for your customers. This powerful plugin lets you display all available variations of a product with different designs, right on the same page, eliminating the need for customers to navigate through multiple pages or websites.

Not only does this feature provide ultimate convenience for your customers, but it's also a powerful sales booster for your store. Customers will love being able to quickly and easily find the perfect color and design, leading to increased customer satisfaction and ultimately higher sales.

With easy installation and no complicated settings required, this plugin is the perfect solution for any WooCommerce store looking to elevate the customer experience. 

N.B: that this feature currently only works on single product pages.
